    Description

    Team Saiyan is build around characters that are Saiyans. This team has almost unlimited build path due to how many characters they are getting. Here is the recommendation of the team.


    Ultra Turles
    Advantages
  • The tankiest character in the current meta.
  • Able to heal a lot of his health back through his abilities.
  • Able to reduce enemy's dragon ball multiple times.
  • Able to restore vanish fully through main ability.
  • Access to blast armor moves.
  • Special Move could nulify unfavorable Element for a short period of time after you enchanced it.
  • Decent combo potential.
  • Access to two health bar by having the "comeback" mechanic.
  • Removes enemy vanish after procing "comeback"
  • Excels at both offense and defence.

    • Disadvantages
  • Have no ways of protecting himself aside from tanking it raw.
    • Lf Tree of Might Goku
    Advantages
  • Unique gauge allows him to heal a lot of his health back while disrupting the enemy's combo.
  • Able to reduce enemy's dragon ball multiple times.
  • Able to restore vanish fully through his Special Move and Ultimate Arts.
  • Access to blast armor moves.
  • Strong combo potential.
  • Able to disrupt enemy's attack even on standby,acting as a hidden defender in the team
  • Special Arts allows him to extend his combos.
  • Able to draw another Ultimate Arts once there is a defeated battle member.
  • The first character to have an ability to counter the "No switching" effect.

    • Disadvantages
  • Requires a dead ally to maximize his damage.
  • Require to tank a few hits to activate his unique gauge, making him weak to characters who excels at burst damage.
    • Ultra Majin Vegeta
    Advantages
  • Locks enemy for two timer count when switch in
  • Unique gauge allows him to counter Blast Arts card anytime when it is filled
  • Fill up Unique gauge very fast
  • Lock enemy in when using Ultimate Arts
  • Ultimate Arts does AOE damage to enemy team
  • Gain nulify "endurance" buff effect for 20 timer counts after using Ultimate Arts
  • Gain Ki and a new card when enemy switches
  • Nulify unfavorable Element after using Special Arts card when Unique Gauge is full
  • Draws a Special Move when Unique gauge is activated,allowing free hits
  • Heals a lot for the first time his health falls below 50%

    • Disadvantages
  • Unique gauge is easier to bypass
  • Unable to nulify unfavorable Element after Unique gauge is used up
    • Ultra Ssj4 Gogeta
    Advantages
  • Excellent combo potential
  • Restore vanish with Special move(Effect increases based on number of cards destroy)
  • Draw 2 cards from Special Arts,allowing longer combos
  • Unique gauge fully puts a stop to enemy combo once filled up
  • Destroy all of enemy desk while drawing at most 4 new cards when procing the unique gauge.
  • Highest burst damage in the game with Ultimate Arts
  • Reduce 3 enemy dragon balls
  • Nulify unfavourable Element from main ability
  • Heals himself each time he filled up his Unique gauge
  • Best last man standing character in the game

  • Easy to get sniped by enemy if unique gauge wasn't filled up

  • Fully heal themselves by fusing into Gogeta Blue.

  • Able to regain vanish gauge fully from main ability(Gogeta)
