    • Fusion Warrior

    *The images are not for sales.

    *The option for team members are based on the team being pure of it's tag. You could also put other characters that does not belong to the tag in it. (Just remember to put them in the leader slot to maximize the z ability effect.)

    Description

    Team Fusion Warrior consists of multiple premium characters each major events. This team focus on dealing high amount of burst damage while providing a little utility to their team


    Ultra Ssj4 Gogeta
    Advantages
  • Excellent combo potential
  • Restore vanish with Special move(Effect increases based on number of cards destroy)
  • Draw 2 cards from Special Arts,allowing longer combos
  • Unique gauge fully puts a stop to enemy combo once filled up
  • Destroy all of enemy desk while drawing at most 4 new cards when procing the unique gauge.
  • Highest burst damage in the game with Ultimate Arts
  • Reduce 3 enemy dragon balls
  • Nulify unfavourable Element from main ability
  • Heals himself each time he filled up his Unique gauge
  • Best last man standing character in the game

    • Disadvantages
  • Slightly squisy
  • Easy to get sniped by enemy if unique gauge wasn't filled up
