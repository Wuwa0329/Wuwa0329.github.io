    Description

    Team Movie Saga is build around characters that comes from the original Dragon Ball movies. This team has the option to run high damage build or being bulky and immortal. Here is the recommendation of the team setup.


    Core members

    Ultra Turles
    Advantages
  • The tankiest character in the current meta.
  • Able to heal a lot of his health back through his abilities.
  • Able to reduce enemy's dragon ball multiple times.
  • Able to restore vanish fully through main ability.
  • Access to blast armor moves.
  • Special Move could nulify unfavorable Element for a short period of time after you enchanced it.
  • Decent combo potential.
  • Access to two health bar by having the "comeback" mechanic.
  • Removes enemy vanish after procing "comeback"
  • Excels at both offense and defence.

    • Disadvantages
  • Have no ways of protecting his teammates aside from tanking it raw.
