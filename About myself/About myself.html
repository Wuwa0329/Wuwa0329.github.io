    • About myself

    As a seasoned player with six years of experience in **Dragon Ball Legends (DBL)** PvP, I've encountered a wide range of competitive scenarios that have shaped my gameplay. Over the years, I've honed techniques, mastered various characters, and developed an in-depth understanding of the mechanics that make PvP both challenging and rewarding. The purpose of this website is to share that expertise with newcomers and help them build a solid foundation in PvP combat. Here, beginners will find detailed guides, tips, and strategies designed to improve their gameplay efficiency. Whether you're looking to climb the ranks or simply enjoy PvP with confidence, this site is here to help you.
